About MobileBoil?

MobileBoile starts where Skeleton Boilerplate left it, it adds a new breakpoint of 1440px for a wider and greater visual experience, and inverses the media-query logic into a mobile-first approach.

Three Core Principles

Skeleton is built on three core principles:

  • A Responsive Grid Up To Large Desktops: Elegant scaling from a mobile to tablets to browsers.
  • Fast to Start: It's a tool for rapid development with best practices
  • Style Agnostic: It provides the most basic, beautiful styles, but is meant to be overwritten. Courtesy of Skeleton.
